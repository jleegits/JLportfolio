My Story


I have been living my whole life with aspects of designs and artistic sense seating in my brain.
Struggled for long enough, being unable to decide a right career that suits my aptitude.
Fortunately, after stepping into a coding world and getting used to it, the struggle finally seemed to fade away. Although running into a problem while coding is very common. Instead, it advances my desire to solve the problem and it makes me become more confident and a better developer.

Thanks to my old passions toward fine art, graphic, UX and UI designing, I can now confidently implement those visual ideas and logics to my codings in most practical and beautiful way.
Very excited to share my thoughts and skills to future teams and produce great works out of all.
